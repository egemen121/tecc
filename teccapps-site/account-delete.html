Request account & data deletion

Use this page to request deletion of your Futmap account and associated data without installing the app.

Option A — Email request (fast)

Send us an email from the address linked to your Futmap account. Include the details below.

Email privacy@teccapps.com

We may contact you to verify ownership before completing deletion.

Option B — In-app deletion (instant)

Open the app → Profile → Settings → Delete account. This removes your account directly from within the app.

What will be deleted

  • Account (email/username) and authentication record
  • Inventory, quests, claims, balances

Certain minimal logs may be retained for a limited period for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance as described in our Privacy Policy.
